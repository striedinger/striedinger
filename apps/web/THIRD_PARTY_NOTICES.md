The browser PDF tool redistributes the following components:

- `qpdf-run` 0.2.1, licensed under the MIT License. Its license is shipped beside the runtime as `LICENSE-qpdf-run.txt`.

The runtime assets are generated from installed package contents during `predev` and `prebuild`; they are not loaded from a third-party CDN.
